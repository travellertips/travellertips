Travellertips website directly does not use any private information of our visitors.

Travellertips website uses 3d-party software that might use the data for statistics, marketing and other means. Below you will find the list of used 3d-party software on our website with the links to their Terms and Conditions that you can use for more info. On this page you are also able to revoke the Cookies settings for your browser.

Cookies policy

Travellertips website may use Cookies for the operational and performance reasons. These cookies are not used to collect any data from our users. However, cookies can be set by the 3d-party software that is used on the Website.

3d-party software used for Travellertips

Current list of the used 3d-party software:

  • Google Analytics. Please read How Google uses data when you use our partners for more info. You can also check Google Analytics policies to get the information about used data.

  • Google Maps services. Please read Google Maps/Google Earth Additional Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policies

  • Cookiebot

  • AddThis Tools. Please refer to their Privacy policies for more information. You're also able to Opt-Out targeting directly on their website.
