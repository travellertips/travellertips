I trapped to this great photo-spot accidentally during my trip to the german city Schwerin.

{% asset schwerin1.jpg alt="Schwerin Land museum" %}

To find this photo-treasure you should go to the small Land-museum (anchor) near Schwerin. If you travel by car — there is no problem to navigate there. You can park the car directly around the corner from the entrance. If you travel by transport, simply take the Bus # from Schwerin.

{% include map.html query="Hechingen-Boll%2C+Mariazell" place_id="ChIJ5apUM6YDmkcRzwwm8OualSE" img="maps/hohenzollern_mariazell.png" %}

You can get a permission of making drone-photography at the entrance if you assure of non-commercial usage.

The museum itself is a very nice place to be. + (description of the museum).

{% asset schwerin3.jpg alt="Schwerin Land museum" %}

You allowed even make a small picnic (of course without fire) on its territory. There is small cafe, where you can taste homemade food and pastry. To be honest — the place doesn’t look like a touristic spot — and this makes it so nice ;)

If you go further opposite to the entrance you will come to the shore of the huge lake — you will notice the ship right away.

{% asset schwerin2.jpg alt="Schwerin Land museum" %}

It’s a great spot for abandoned places lovers.

Traveller tips

When is the best time to visit

I would say that the best time is summer, as you can have a picnic on the grass and swim in the lake.
